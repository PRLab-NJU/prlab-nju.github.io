  en: |
    <p>Xiaoming Li is a tenure-track Associate Professor at the School of Intelligent Science and Technology, Nanjing University. He received his B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology, with a joint Ph.D. training at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He subsequently worked as a Research Fellow at MMLab@NTU, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research interests primarily focus on image and video generation, editing, and restoration. In recent years, he has achieved a series of advances in domain-specific scenarios such as face and text processing, with several research outcomes successfully deployed in real-world applications by domestic and international enterprises, including mobile device companies. He has published over 20 papers in leading journals and conferences such as TPAMI, IJCV, CVPR, ICCV, and ECCV, and has been granted more than 10 domestic and international patents. He currently leads the Excellent Young Scientists Fund (Overseas). His research group collaborates closely with industry partners including vivo, Alibaba, and Huawei. The group is actively recruiting Ph.D. students, Master’s students, research assistants, and interns. Highly motivated candidates with strong interest in image/video restoration, editing, and generative models are warmly welcomed. Please contact via email for inquiries.
    </p>
