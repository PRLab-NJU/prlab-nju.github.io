    <p>I am a tenure-track Associate Professor at the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Nanjing University. Prior to this, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020 and subsequently worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.</p><p>My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and medical data analysis, with the goal of developing intelligent systems to advance clinical decision-making and healthcare delivery. To date, I have published over 40 papers in leading international journals and conferences.
